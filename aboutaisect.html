About
ASIECT Organization
( Empowering Education, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship for a Better Tomorrow )

AISECT is a top Indian social enterprise focused on skill development, higher education, financial inclusion, e-governance, and other ICT services in semi-urban and rural areas. Since 1985, the Bhopal-based organization has tirelessly served remote regions for 37 years, empowering people, creating jobs for youth, and promoting entrepreneurship. 

The refined over time AISECT model is self-sustaining, scalable, and entrepreneurial, seamlessly meeting the demands of local communities for various skills and ICT services. It has a wide reach, operating in 605 districts, 2100 blocks, and 8550 Panchayats across 29 states and 4 territories through over 31000 centers and offices. Till date, we’ve trained over 26 lakh people, created jobs for over 100,000, and improved the lives of over 50 lakh people through innovative products and services.
