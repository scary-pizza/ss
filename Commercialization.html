What is
Commercialization
( Collaborating for Innovation, Research, and Impact )

The Process of Commercialization at Shodh Shikhar is designed to transform innovative research and groundbreaking ideas into real-world products and solutions. It involves a structured approach that bridges the gap between concept and market. Starting with idea generation and validation, researchers and innovators work on shaping their concepts into practical solutions. This is followed by prototype development, testing, and refinement to ensure feasibility and effectiveness. Once validated, the process moves towards intellectual property protection, including patent filing, to safeguard innovation. Through strategic collaborations with industries, research institutions, and partner organizations, we facilitate technology transfer and provide necessary resources for scaling. Finally, the innovation enters the market deployment phase, where it reaches end-users, creating social, technological, and economic impact. At Shodh Shikhar, we ensure that every step of this process is supported by expert mentorship, strong partnerships, and access to opportunities that empower innovators to turn their vision into reality.
