About
Partner’s
( Collaborating for Innovation, Research, and Impact )

Shodh Shikhar stands as one of India’s most prestigious research and innovation festivals, celebrated for fostering creativity, collaboration, and groundbreaking discoveries. The success of this grand initiative is built upon the strong Commercializations we share with esteemed organisations, universities, industries, research councils, and innovation-driven institutions across the country.
These collaborations are more than just associations — they represent a shared vision of progress and a commitment to shaping the future of research and technology. By combining knowledge, resources, and expertise, our Commercializations help create a powerful ecosystem where young innovators, researchers, and academicians come together to exchange ideas, develop solutions, and bring transformative projects to life.

Through their support, Shodh Shikhar has been able to bridge the gap between academia and industry, inspire cutting-edge innovations, and provide an influential platform where aspiring minds gain global exposure. Together, we are nurturing a culture of collaboration, learning, and excellence, ensuring that the next generation of leaders is well-equipped to address real-world challenges and drive India toward a Viksit Bharat.
