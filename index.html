About the summit
Advancing Green Technologies and SDGs For Vikist Bharat

The 5th Shodh Shikhar Vigyan Parv, a flagship initiative of the AISECT Group of Universities, is dedicated to advancing research, innovation, and sustainable development in alignment with the vision of Viksit Bharat. The 2026 edition will place particular emphasis on green technologies, sustainable practices, and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), while offering structured opportunities for research publication, project exhibitions, and idea commercialization.
