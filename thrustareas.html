Thrust Areas
( Empowering Education, Innovation, and Entrepreneurship for a Better Tomorrow )
Overview

At Shodh Shikhar, our thrust areas define the core domains of innovation and research that drive sustainable growth, cutting-edge discoveries, and impactful solutions. These areas are strategically curated to foster interdisciplinary collaboration and nurture transformative ideas that address real-world challenges. By covering a wide spectrum—from emerging technologies and scientific advancements to socio-economic development—we aim to bridge the gap between research and implementation. Through this platform, we bring together academicians, industry experts, innovators, and thought leaders, creating a dynamic ecosystem where knowledge meets application. Our focus spans diverse sectors, ensuring that every participant—be it a researcher, student, policymaker, or entrepreneur—finds opportunities for learning, collaboration, and contributing to meaningful change.


Themes

Shodh Shikhar 2025 brings together diverse domains of innovation, research, and knowledge sharing through its six key tracks. These tracks are designed to foster collaboration, encourage groundbreaking discoveries, and provide a platform for researchers, academicians, professionals, and innovators to showcase their contributions:
